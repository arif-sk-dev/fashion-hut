Return Policy

Overview

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not satisfied, we gladly accept returns within 30 days of delivery, subject to the conditions below.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition.
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required.
  • Sale and clearance items are non-returnable.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Steps to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at returns@yourstore.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Repackage the item securely with original materials.
  3. Ship to:
    [Your Return Address Here]
  4. You'll receive confirmation and refund within 7–10 business days of item receipt.

Exchanges

To exchange an item, indicate your desired product in your return request. We'll ship the replacement once we receive your return.

Need Help?

Questions? Reach out anytime at support@yourstore.com. We’re here to help.
